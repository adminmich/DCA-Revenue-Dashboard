Key Insights

Methodology. Source: NMI transactions export (—). Customers are grouped by email (with a name+card fallback). A customer is classified active if their most recent successful charge falls within 35 days of the latest transaction in the dataset, and churned otherwise. Tenure for churned members is computed as (last paid − first paid) + 30 days (the trailing payment covers the next billing cycle); tenure for active members is as-of date − first paid. Averages exclude in-progress active members from the "average tenure" figure unless explicitly labelled; the headline number is the average tenure of churned members, which represents the actual time a typical customer stays before cancelling.
